My favourite movies

These are the movies I like the most

These are the movies I like the most. I have seen them many times and I never get tired of them.

Spirited Away

This is a well-known movie by Studio Ghibli (Hayao Miyazaki). It is a story about criticism of capitalism and the importance of family and non forgetting who you are.

Arrietty

This is the most beautiful movie I have ever seen. The pictures are really fantastic and the story is very touching. Also, the music by Cecile Corbel is amazing.

The Wind Rises

Very touching story about the life of Jiro Horikoshi, the designer of the Zero fighter plane. It is a story about dreams and the reality of life. The story between Jiro and Nahoko is very touching.

Harry Potter

I love all the Harry Potter movies. I have read all the books and I have seen all the movies. I love the story and the characters. I think it is a very well written story.
